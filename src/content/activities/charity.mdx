## Our Charity Fundraiser

Every year, furry conventions raise impressive amounts of money for charities around the world, and we will be no exception. Watch this space as we finalise the shortlist of charities we will be working to support in 2027 and plan how to bring them to the convention to showcase what they do and what your donations will mean to them.
