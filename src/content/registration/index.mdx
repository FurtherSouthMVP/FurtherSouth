## Ticket Options

To register at the convention, you will need to purchase a ticket of one of the following types (note as we've not finalised the registration process for 2027, this is all tentative!):

**Day Passes** – allow you to choose one day of the event and visit all activities during that time. There is internal discussions on going about a pass that can be purchased on the day to let you into the convention as well (more to be announced).

**Attendance Tickets** – allow you to visit the venue hotel throughout each of the four days of the convention and enjoy all of the activities throughout.

**Residential Tickets** – includes your overnight stay at the hotel, with breakfast in addition to the Attendance ticket. Plentiful shared double or twin rooms are available, while a limited set of single rooms can be booked. As well, check out our new offering, triple bedrooms, which let you share with both a double bed and sofa-bed.
