## Ticket Options

To register at the convention, you will need to purchase a ticket of one of the following types:

**Day Passes** – allow you to choose one day of the event and visit all activities during that time. There are also internal discussions ongoing about a pass that can be purchased on the day to let you into the convention for that day (more to be announced).

**Attendance Tickets** – allow you to visit the venue hotel throughout each of the four days of the convention and enjoy all of the activities throughout.

**Residential Tickets** – this year, hotel accommodation is booked **separately** and is not included with your ticket. Attendees can take advantage of a discounted rate at the Holiday Inn Southsea, with a range of room options available, including shared double or twin rooms, a limited number of single rooms, and triple rooms featuring a double bed and sofa bed. Your ticket includes event attendance only.

## Opening Dates

Registration will be open from the 1st of June. Make sure to register early to get a discounted rate on your ticket and to ensure you get a space in the main hotel!
